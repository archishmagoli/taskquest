## Reflection

### 1. What went well during this unit?

Planning the app concept went smoothly. The gamification angle — earning points from completed tasks and spending them to unlock avatars — gave the app a clear identity and made it easy to derive user stories and features naturally from that core loop. Writing the feature list and user stories felt cohesive because everything ties back to that central idea, and I felt motivated to complete everything because this was an application I myself could use on a daily basis.

### 2. What were some challenges your group faced in this unit?

My partner for the project didn't respond in time, so working solo made it harder to get a sense of whether I was going in the right direction. Deciding on the right scope — enough features to meet all the requirements without overbuilding for Milestone 1 — took some careful thought, as well, especially since I wanted to ensure the planned features map to both baseline and custom feature requirements.

### 3. What additional support will you need in upcoming units as you continue to work on your final project?

I will probably need guidance on designing the database schema to support the points and avatar unlocking system, particularly the many-to-many relationship between users and avatars (a user can unlock many avatars, an avatar can be unlocked by many users). I would also want support on connecting the points logic on the backend to real-time UI updates on the frontend. Some advice on design would be great, as well - I don't have a keen eye for making things look pretty/cohesive on the UI/UX end.
